Event Schedule

Two days of intense robotics competitions and activities

9:30 AM - 12:30 PM

Fast Line Follower

Test your robot's speed and precision in our challenging line following course. Single elimination tournament with prizes for the top three finishers.

12:30 PM - 1:30 PM

Lunch Break

Refuel and recharge for the afternoon competitions. Food and beverages will be provided for all participants.

1:30 PM - 5:30 PM

Robowars

The ultimate battle of robots! Watch as custom-built machines face off in our combat arena. May the best bot win!

5:30 PM - 6:30 PM

Snacks Break

A short break to grab some snacks and network with fellow robotics enthusiasts.

6:30 PM - 8:30 PM

Robosoccer

Experience the future of sports with our autonomous soccer competition. Single elimination tournament with exciting matches.

8:30 PM Onwards

Wrap-up & Dinner

Join us for dinner as we celebrate the day's competitions and announce the winners. Followed by accommodation arrangements.
