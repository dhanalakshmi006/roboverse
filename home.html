ROBOWARS

RoboWars is a high-energy competition where teams design and build combat robots to battle in an enclosed arena. Each match challenges participants to showcase mechanical strength, strategic design, and precise control as robots compete to overpower their opponents. Beyond the clashes, RoboWars celebrates innovation, teamwork, and engineering excellence under pressure.

2025

ROBOSOCCER

RoboSoccer brings soccer to life through robotics, where teams build autonomous or remote-controlled robots to pass, defend, and score on a mini soccer field. The competition tests coordination, strategy, and real-time decision-making as robots sense their environment, respond to opponents, and work together. RoboSoccer highlights teamwork, innovation, and intelligent robotic design in action.

2025

FLF

FLF (Fast Line Follower) is a high-speed robotics challenge where teams build fully autonomous robots to follow a track with maximum speed and precision. The event tests sensor accuracy, control algorithms, and mechanical balance as robots navigate curves and recover from errors without slowing down. FLF celebrates refined engineering, where intelligence, precision, and speed determine success.
